About

California Stop. is a solo instrumental rock project from Wichita, Kansas. Drawing inspiration from post-rock bands like Explosions in the Sky and This Will Destroy You, California Stop. weaves together melodies to create dense and dynamic polyphonic textures. The sound is defined by its reverb-soaked instrumentation, shimmering guitars, and dynamic arrangements ranging from softer, melancholy qualities that evolve into a dense wall of sound. California Stop. uses these techniques to convey emotion and tell a story exclusively through instrumentation.
